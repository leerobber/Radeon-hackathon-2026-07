Six tools

Real computation, not templates

VcfAnalyzer

Real chi-square Hardy-Weinberg QC per variant, exact df=1 identity, plus the worst-fitting SNP's real genotype counts.

LdBlock

Pairwise Pearson r² linkage disequilibrium, union-find block detection across the cohort.

HaplotypeTool

Phased haplotype frequency tallying across adjacent-SNP windows.

PopulationStructure

GPU correlation matrix → CPU PCA → ancestry clustering, with a bootstrap CI on PC1.

LdConfidence

GPU-batched bootstrap 95% CI on the strongest observed LD estimate.

SelectionScan

Per-SNP Wright's FST with a real 200-permutation significance test.
